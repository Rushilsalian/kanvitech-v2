FAQ  (also supports SEO)
----------------------------------------------------------
Q: What does Tally implementation include?
A: We install and configure Tally to match how your business works - setting it up correctly so you're ready to use it from day one.

Q: Do you offer onsite support, or only remote?
A: Both. Depending on your plan and the issue, we help remotely for quick fixes and visit onsite when needed.

Q: What is a Tally AMC?
A: An Annual Maintenance Contract gives you ongoing Tally support for the year - covering troubleshooting, assistance and upkeep so you're never stuck.

Q: Can you help run Tally across multiple branches?
A: Yes. We set up data synchronization and multi-branch management so your locations stay connected with real-time access.

Q: Do you train our team on Tally?
A: Yes. We provide TallyPrime training so your staff can work confidently and make the most of the software.

Q: How is this different from Tally Software Services (TSS)?
A: TSS is Tally's own subscription that keeps your licence updated. Tally Services is our hands-on support - setup, troubleshooting, AMC and training. (See our Tally Software Services page.)
