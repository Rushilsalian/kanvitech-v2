WHAT IT IS
----------------------------------------------------------
Spine Payroll is payroll software that takes the month-end pressure off your HR team. It handles salary calculations, payslips, deductions and statutory compliance automatically, with all your employee and payroll data in one place. What used to take days of manual work and double-checking now runs accurately in a fraction of the time.


----------------------------------------------------------
WHO IT'S FOR  /  USE CASE
----------------------------------------------------------
For any business that runs a monthly payroll and wants it done right - from small teams to large companies across every industry. It's ideal if you're still calculating salaries on spreadsheets, struggling to keep up with PF, ESIC and tax rules, or simply want payroll that's quick, accurate and compliant without the stress.

FAQ  (also supports SEO)
----------------------------------------------------------
Q: What does Spine Payroll do?
A: It automates your entire payroll - salary calculations, payslips, deductions and statutory compliance - so your monthly payroll runs accurately with minimal manual effort.

Q: Does it handle PF, ESIC and tax compliance?
A: Yes. Spine Payroll takes care of statutory compliance including PF, ESIC, PT, TDS, Gratuity, Bonus and LWF, with tax tables that stay up to date as rules change.

Q: Can it manage employee loans and advances?
A: Yes. You can set up loans and advances with automatic repayment tracking, so deductions are handled correctly each cycle.

Q: Can it handle arrears and backdated payments?
A: Yes. Spine Payroll processes arrears, adjustments and backdated payments smoothly, without delays or manual rework.

Q: Is there a mobile app?
A: Yes - employees and admins can access payroll and HR data through the Spine HR mobile app on Android and iOS.

Q: How is this different from Spine HR Suite?
A: Spine Payroll focuses on salary processing and compliance. Spine HR Suite is the full HRMS - recruitment, attendance, performance and more - with payroll as part of it. (See our Spine HR Suite page.)
